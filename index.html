About

We are YR Media's music distribution and production company for up and coming young creatives. Based out of Oakland, Ca we focus on developing young artists, songwriters, producers, and engineers from the Bay. We provide A&R consulting, artist marketing and promo services, as well as an outlet for these artists to distribute their music to the world and help them to perfect their crafts. We also put out fire music so tap inwardly!
